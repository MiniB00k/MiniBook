About Us
 As human beings, all of us love to keep things. I personally have found my joy in keeping coins. Even when we started keeping gold and diamonds, we chose to keep them behind locked doors, just to make sure no one could take them. And 5,500 years ago, we finally were able to keep and our most precious thing. We started holding pencils and keeping our thoughts. What makes them so precious is that, unlike gold, no one can prevent us from keeping them or take them from us.

What I’m trying to do is to bring back that idea to life, to respect the people who spent their whole life trying to share their stories. I was frustrated to hear the “I have no time to read” answer, so our goal is to give you a 5-minute summary of their beautiful stories.
